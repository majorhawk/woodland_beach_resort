We are only 99 miles from 694 and 494 junction, less than a tank of gas round trip.
Come enjoy a traditional Minnesota family vacation at Woodland Beach Resort on Bay Lake.

Making vacation memories for 81 years. Woodland Beach Resort is Located on beautiful Bay Lake, a 2,392 acre lake filled with bays, points and islands. Bay Lake is one of the top Bass fishing lakes in Minnesota. A short 99 mile drive from the Twin Cities brings you to the heart of the lakes area. We are located 10 minutes from Mille Lacs Lake. 20 minutes from Grand Casino and 20 minutes from Brainerd. A variety of golf courses, tennis courts, restaurants, movies, amusement parks, museums and shopping are all a short drive away.

Enjoy the world class 25-mile mountain bike trails, covering 800 acres in the Cuyuna's County State Recreation Area near Crosby. Other activities in the Cuyuna's County State Recreation Area include scuba diving and fishing for Rainbow and Brook Trout.
