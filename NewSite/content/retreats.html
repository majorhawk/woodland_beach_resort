Retreat at Woodland Beach Resort on Bay Lake

Do you need some uninterrupted time to finish a project, or do you have a project that you have been dreaming of starting for years?

Do you just need some time to relax with your pals?


Woodland Beach Resort nestled along Bay Lake is the perfect place to hold a retreat where you can relax, create, play, and share time with friends.

Types of Gatherings

The Retreat Lodge

The 1200 sq. ft. Retreat Lodge built in 2007 will comfortably hold up to 18 people for a craft retreat, and it will hold more people for a reunion or similar function. The well-lit lodge has its own stair free entrance for easy loading and unloading of supplies. The lodge also features several other amenities to make your retreat more comfortable including:

  • A 6' table for each guest (up to 18 people)
  • Task lighting, power strips, and waste baskets
  • 102" projection TV with surround sound that is compatible with DVD, VHS, iPod, and CDs
  • Upstairs loft allows guests to spread out
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Snack kitchen with refrigerator/freezer, sink, microwave, coffeepot, and plenty of counter space for serving
  • Restroom
  • Heated floors on main level

Accommodations

The resort has seven cabins built since 2005 that are available year round with Cabin #18 conveniently attached to the spacious Retreat Lodge. This cabin is ideal for guests that have trouble getting around because they can enjoy the retreat and sleep without using any stairs. All of the cabins have a fully equipped kitchen, stone gas fireplace, in-floor heat on main level, TV, sitting area, and screen porch or deck. Please note that bed linens and pillows are provided, but towels, soap, and dishrags are not included.

Contact us about rates for extra nights and additional information.

Dining

There is a snack kitchen in the lodge that has a refrigerator/freezer, sink, microwave, coffeepot, and plenty of counter space for serving, so you can create meals for your guests, have a potluck, or order takeout or delivery. Each cabin has a fully equipped kitchen including stove, microwave, full-size refrigerator, and dishwasher. The cabins are also stocked with pots, pans, plates, silverware, and other essential dishes. There is a grocery store in Garrison just 8 miles away from the resort for last minute meal essentials. Please call us for more information on catering or delivery options.

Things to Bring

  • Food and Drinks (alcohol, if used responsibly is permitted)
  • Storage containers to transport leftovers
  • Dish Rags and Dish Soap
  • Clothes, Toiletry Bag, and Towels
  • Walking Shoes
  • CDs, DVDs, and Videos
  • Craft Projects and Supplies
  • Camera
