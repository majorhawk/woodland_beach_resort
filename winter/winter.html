Woodland Beach Resort has great winter fun!!!

We have seven newer cabins open year round. Cabins 9 and 11 have two bedrooms, one bath. Cabins 10, 12, 18 and 20 have four bedrooms, two full baths. Cabin 19 has two bedrooms down and a lofted area with four twins and two full baths. All have stone gas fireplaces, in-floor heating on lower level, and full kitchen with dishwasher, vaulted ceilings in 10, 12 and 19 and much more.

Some winter fun includes snowmobiling (Bay Lake is part of the Turtle Lake) Trail, skating, cross-country skiing, snow shoeing, winter Fat Tire Biking at Cuyuna County State, Recreation Area, 20 miles of groomed trails dedicated to fat biking, rated biginner through expert and ice fishing or just relax by the fire. Ask about renting our 6’ -6” x 14’-0” aluminum fish house. Come enjoy some great winter fun at Woodland Beach Resort on Bay Lake!!!
