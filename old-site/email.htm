Thanks for your interest in Woodland Beach Resort.
Bay Lake is one of Minnesota's premier lakes with
14 bays, 24 miles of shoreline, 2400 acres of water
and only 18 miles east of Brainerd. Our housekeeping
cabins range from the classic cabins built in the 1930's
to new luxury accommodations. The "Reunion Center",
new in 2005, can accommodate up to 32 people.
We specialize in family reunions!

During your visit to Woodland Beach Resort you will
enjoy world class fishing, boating and swimming,
many nearby golf courses, local dining, hiking,
amusement parks, biking, casinos, antiquing and
many shopping opportunities. For more information
visit our website at www.WoodlandBeachResort.com.,
