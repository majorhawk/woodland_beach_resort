ADDITIONAL WEEKLY GUEST $125.00 PER PERSON/PER WEEK.

DAY VISITORS / OVERNIGHT GUESTS
We must be notified prior to arrival the names and number of day visitors and overnight guests. and length of stay. Day Guest: $10.00 per person / per dayOvernight Guest: $25.00 per person / per night (over age 2)We reserve the right to limit or refuse visitors.

DEPOSITS
A Deposit of 50% of rental rate per cabin and extra such as boats, pontoons, pets and boat slips. Groups that rent 3 or more cabins need to contact resort office for deposit information. Deposits can be refunded up to 50% only if the reservation is cancelled more than 90 days prior to arrival date and we are able to re-rent the cabin.

MISCELLANEOUS CHARGES
Pontoons and Fishing Boats available for rent.

Paddle Boards/Kayaks........Free to use to Woodland Beach Resort Guests Only

Paddle Boat........Free to use to Woodland Beach Resort Guests Only
Canoe........Free to use to Woodland Beach Resort Guests Only
Boat slip (1)........$35.00 per day or $60.00 per week. All larger boats require prior approval.
Let us know size of boat.
Pontoon slip (1)........$70.00 per day or $120.00 per week. All pontoons require prior approval.
Let us know size of pontoon.


PET CHARGES
$85.00 per pet per week or $15.00 per pet per night and must sign pet agreement
We reserve the right to refuse pets.


CHECK IN/CHECK OUT TIMES
Check in time is 3:00pm
Check out time is 9:00am
Early arrivals-Late departures will be charged $50.00 per hour.


NIGHTLY STAYS 3 night minimum stay.
Stays of less than 3 nights will be assessed a 25% additional charge per night.

All prices subject to change. All prices subject to 6.875% Minnesota Sales Tax and .5% County Tax.
